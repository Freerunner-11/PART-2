Mr. Lie

Part of the exclusive Little Mister Collection — created by none other than Dr. Wondertainment.

Appearance

Mr. Lie appears as a friendly, well-dressed young man with a mischievous twinkle in his eye. He looks trustworthy at first glance, but there’s always something slightly “off” about his smile.

Personality

True to his name, Mr. Lie is physically incapable of telling the truth. Instead, he communicates using riddles, half-truths, and rhetorical questions — leading others to draw their own (often incorrect) conclusions. He can be frustrating, but always entertaining.

Special Notes

  • #17 in the Little Misters series
  • A tattoo reading "Mr. Lie, from Little Misters ® by Dr. Wondertainment" is visible on his left arm.
  • Warning: Do not attempt games such as "Truth or Dare" with Mr. Lie. The results may be unpredictable.
