Mr. Smile

Part of the exclusive Little Mister Collection — created by none other than Dr. Wondertainment.

Appearance

Mr. Smile was designed as a cheerful companion toy, always wearing a bright grin. His colorful design and charming presence made him an early favorite prototype in the Little Misters line.

Personality

As his name suggests, Mr. Smile was meant to bring joy and positivity wherever he went. He radiated happiness — sometimes a little too much.

Special Notes

  • #01 — Prototype Model
  • A tattoo reading "Mr. Smile, from Little Misters ® by Dr. Wondertainment" appears on his right hand.
  • Status: Discontinued. The prototype was deemed defective and was lost at sea during shipping trials. Mr. Smile is no longer in circulation.
