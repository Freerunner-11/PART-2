Mr. Forgetful

Part of the exclusive Little Mister Collection — created by none other than Dr. Wondertainment.

Appearance

Mr. Forgetful looks like a young adult human male. He always seems slightly distracted, with a distant look in his eyes, as if he’s forgotten what he was just doing.

Personality

Mr. Forgetful is kind-hearted but suffers from constant lapses in memory. He struggles to remember names, conversations, or even where he left his shoes — which makes every interaction with him a little unpredictable. Despite this, he’s easygoing and fun to be around… if you don't mind repeating yourself.

Special Notes

  • #04 in the Little Misters series
  • A tattoo reading "Mr. Forgetful, from Little Misters ® by Dr. Wondertainment" is located on his right calf.
  • Sometimes forgets that he's part of the Little Misters collection entirely.
