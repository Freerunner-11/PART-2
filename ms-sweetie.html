Ms. Sweetie

Part of the exclusive Little Mister Collection — created by none other than Dr. Wondertainment.

Appearance

Ms. Sweetie is a bright, candy-themed young lady with pink hair, a sugary smile, and an outfit that looks like it's made out of gumdrops and cotton candy. Wherever she goes, she smells faintly of caramel and strawberries.

Personality

As sweet as her name suggests! Ms. Sweetie is friendly, bubbly, and always eager to share kindness. Sometimes her sweetness can feel overwhelming, but she always means well.

Special Notes
